ABOUT US

Hai we are exam seating arrangment system. we are here to help you. The purpose of developing exam hall seating arrangement system is to computerized the traditional way of conducting exams and help staffs in allocating exam hall easily without any burden. Another purpose of developing this software is to generate the report automatically during exams at the end of the session or in between the session. This project also allocate particular invigilator for particular hall. It is also very useful for the college where the software may generate the hall separation. Hence the hall is allocated to the students automatically based on their departments and register numbers.
